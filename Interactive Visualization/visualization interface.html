Sentiment Analysis On Music Lyrics: Interactive Visualization

Welcome to our Interactive Visualization demo page. As shown below, there are 3 different options available to you for viewing a ~15,000 record subset of our result data that was compiled for this demo: 1) General Exploration 2) Artist-Specific 3) Top Scores.

1) General Exploration:

For general exploration purposes, we have taken randomized subsets of classification results from each of our experiments. Each of the randomized subsets contain 500 individual records that correspond to each of the classifications that were made in the experiments (i.e. positive, negative, happy, sad, etc.). Both the spaCy and Clustering demos use the same song records in order to allow for a better visual comparison of how the different methods classified the same songs into their respective categories. To view the results, please click on the select box shown below and choose your desired option.



2) Artist-Specific:

A list of 30 artists/groups from a variety of different time periods and genres are included in our sample dataset. To view the emotional classifications of these songs, as determined via our clustering experiment, please click on the select box shown below and choose your desired option.



3) Top Scores:

The "top" 100 scores from our initial experiments involving VADER and spaCy are included in our sample dataset. These scores include the top 100 highest and lowest compound, valence, and arousal values that were produced for the entirety of our ~250,000 song records. These correspond to the top positive, negative, happy, sad, angry, and relaxed scores. To view the top VADER/spaCy classifications and scores, please click on the select box shown below and choose your desired option.
